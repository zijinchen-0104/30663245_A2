Australia's Property Evaluation

This page shows the distribution of Australian property distribution and evaluations.

Australian property trends over time

From the above area chart, we can see that Australia's property prices fluctuated from September 2018 to January 2021 and gradually increased. Property prices were the lowest around January of each year but then showed an upward trend. The highest value was in March 2021, with an average price of $940839.45.

Next, let us analyze the impact of multiple factors on property prices.

Australian property distribution

The right-hand-side map compares the property price trends among five states in Australia, i.e., Victoria, Queensland, New South Wales, South Australia and Western Australia. The color goes from lighter to darker, which means the price is getting higher and higher. We can see that New South Wales has the highest property price and South Australia has the lowest property price.

Property type among each city VS Price

From the left stacked bar chart, we can know that Sydney's properties have the highest price among all Australian cities, the second one is Melbourne. The number annotations on the chart show the average properties price in each city. It also shows that the house prices are highest and the unit prices are lowest, no matter in which city.

City and Time VS Price

The right-hand-side line chart shows the trend of changes in average property prices between different cities over time. Sydney is the number one city in property prices, far higher than other cities, followed by Melbourne. The lowest property price is in Adelaide. Sydney and Melbourne property prices fluctuated significantly, while other cities were relatively stable.

Bedroom size and Time VS Price

The heatmap on the left shows the effect of different bedroom sizes on housing prices over time. We know that the prices of a house with 5 bedrooms are the highest, and the prices of a house with 2 to 4 bedrooms are almost the same. From January to February of each year, the average prices of all bedroom sizes are relatively low, while from September to November are relatively high.
